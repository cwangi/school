About us

Bal Uddhar Secondary School (BUSS, +2) is located at Budhanilkantha metropolitancity, Kathmandu, in Bagmati Zone which was established in 20..? B.S (A.D) with the help of local residents. This school being one of the leading community schools of the area, has been playing a great role in the development of quality education. It has been able to maintain its high esteem in the community because of its perfect management, quality education as per the requirement of time, highly qualified, experienced and dedicated teachers team as well as loving and helpful non-teaching staffs. There are around fifteen hundred (1500) students from playgroup to +2 level. Students of around forty (40) different districts are obtaining quality education in this school. It is one of the leading community schools of the area. Many economically back warded as well as the students from the remote area are studying in this school. B.U.S.S offers both General (Grade 1 to 12) and Technical Education (Grade 9 to 12). In general, we have classes from ECD to Grade 12 and in Technical stream, we offer Computer Engineering classes from Grade 9 to 12. Along with Computer Engineering; we offer Science, Management, Education, Humanities, and Law faculties in +2 levels. We have 24/7 CC TV surveillance at School. Government has awarded us as Model school of the area in the year 2076 B.S. 1 teaching and 1 non-teaching staffs are working in the school at present.

This school has a true vision of inculcating quality education according to the need of time for the holistic development of the students. We work to make our students well-integrated person; spiritually, socially, intellectually, morally and culturally. We are helping them to be aware of their dignity and for the right use of liberty. Our vision is to prepare globally competent individuals by encouraging them to become responsible, reflective and empowered learners. We envision our graduate leaves the school with following attributes:
* Competence and confidence
* A balance of fundamental skills and a set of spiritual and moral values
* Patience
* Love and respect for others
